Teomic Fire

Electronic / High Energy Duo

TEOMIC FIRE is a British electronic duo consisting of twin brothers Lucas and Finn Van den Berg, born on August 2, 2004. The two elevate the telepathic synergy between them to create a futuristic and unique soundscape.

The duo is known for their debut single "Binary Flame," in which they expertly blend deep atmospheric synthesizers with pulsing rhythms. They represent a refreshing frequency in the modern trance scene, using advanced technology to push the boundaries of the genre.

The aesthetics of Lucas and Finn are characterized by minimalist "tech wear" with LED accents synchronized to the beat of the music. In dark underground clubs, they perform against giant screens projecting digital fire, aiming to create an immersive experience where the audience and sound become one unified pulse. Beyond their music, the brothers are deeply involved in every stage of digital creation - from AI-driven music production, through songwriting and composition, to album cover design and global distribution.
