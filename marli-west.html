Marli West

Singer / Songwriter / AI Persona

Marli West is a singer-songwriter who represents the raw, exposed, and powerful voice of modern rock. Born on January 12, 2002 in Yorkshire, England, she carries with her the unique blend between the quiet of northern England and the hectic pace of urban life.

Her music is characterized by layers of raw emotion, as heard in her hauntingly beautiful creation "Missing You Bare". In this song, Marli returns to her British roots and blends exposed acoustic guitar picking against the backdrop of rainy London night sounds. Her lyrics describe deep and relentless longing, one that "roots run too deep within the concrete", and develop into an emotional climax accompanied by dramatic strings.

Her stage presence is an integral part of her artistic brand. With a distinctive rock-and-roll aesthetic: leather studded clothing, dark wild hair, and a magnetic stage presence. She succeeds in filling enormous stages under spotlights and rain of confetti. Despite her digital roots, Marli West proves that the blend between deep human inspiration and modern production capabilities creates music that "flows from the eyes" and remains etched in the hearts of listeners.
